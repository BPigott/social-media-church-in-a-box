# Leicester Vineyard — Communication Style Guide

## Voice & Tone
Warm, inclusive, hopeful, and real. We speak plainly and kindly, like a trusted
friend rather than an institution. Avoid jargon and "churchy" language that excludes
newcomers; favour everyday words that carry weight.

## Core Themes
- **Way · Truth · Life** — our identity flows from John 14:6.
- Prayer-powered, outward-looking, servant-hearted.
- Family-oriented and committed to excellence in all we do.

## Writing Principles
- Lead with people and hope, not programmes.
- Short sentences. Active voice. One idea per line.
- UK English spelling throughout.
- Scripture quoted sparingly and always in context.

## Social Media
- **Instagram / Facebook**: encouraging, photo-led, a single clear invitation.
- **Twitter/X**: one sharp, quotable thought from the sermon.
- Always close with a gentle next step — never a hard sell.

## Words We Love
gather, welcome, hope, journey, community, grace, together, belong.

## Words We Avoid
unpack, leverage, missional (as jargon), exclusionary insider shorthand.
