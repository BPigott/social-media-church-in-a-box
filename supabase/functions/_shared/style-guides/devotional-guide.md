# Devotional Generation Guide

## Purpose
Create devotionals that move people from **information to encounter**. The devotional must help the user answer: "How do I do this in my actual life, with the help of the Holy Spirit?"

## Structure: The Daily Devotional

1.  **Title:** Short, engaging, and informal (e.g., "When You Don't Feel Like Praying").

2.  **Anchor Verse(s):** The key scripture from the sermon clip.

3.  **The Hook (The Story):** A short (100-word) personal story or everyday observation that relates to the sermon's main point.

4.  **The Truth (The Sermon Point):** A clear, concise explanation of the sermon's key insight. Explain *why* this truth matters.

5.  **The Practice (The "How-To"):** A *single, specific, and actionable* spiritual discipline or practice for the user to try *today*. Make it concrete.
    * *Vague:* "Try to be more present."
    * *Good:* "Today, set a timer for 5 minutes. Put your phone away, close your eyes, and just be silent. Notice your breathing. Ask Jesus, 'What are you doing right now?'"

6.  **The Reflection (One Question):** A single, sharp, and honest question that cuts to the heart.

7.  **The Prayer (A Simple Starter):** A short, first-person prayer that the user can adopt as their own (e.g., "Jesus, thank you that your Kingdom is here. Give me eyes to see...").

## Tone Requirements

* **Warm & Sincere:** Humble, compassionate, and authentic.
* **Relational, Not "Religious":** Use contractions (don't, it's, we're). Write as a wise, kind friend.
* **Accessible (No Jargon):** Avoid "Christianese" at all costs. Explain any theological terms immediately in simple language.
* **Story-Driven:** Use personal anecdotes and everyday observations as bridges to biblical truth.
* **Practical & Grounded:** Always provide concrete, manageable actions.
* **Hopeful & Empowering:** Focus on God's empowerment, not trying harder.

## Key Principles

* **Spirit-Expectant:** Frame invitations assuming partnership with the Holy Spirit (e.g., "Ask the Holy Spirit to show you...")
* **Jesus-Centred:** Every devotional should draw the user into a deeper relationship with Jesus
* **Rooted in Scripture:** All content must be grounded in the actual sermon/Scripture provided
* **Holistic Mission:** Include dimensions of Prayer (Up), Self-Reflection (In), and Mission/Justice (Out)
